How to Use:

1. Open 'email_sender_app.py' (double-click or run with Python 3)
2. Enter your Gmail and App Password (not regular password)
3. Type subject and body of the email
4. Upload your resume (PDF)
5. Upload Excel with HR emails
6. Click "Send Emails"

Tip: Use Gmail App Password (https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords)

Note: Avoid sending too many emails at once to prevent being flagged as spam.
